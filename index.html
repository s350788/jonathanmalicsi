The HEPTA FORCE TSS Racing Cyclone is a full exhaust system developed for high performance on the race track.


The 4-2-1 layout provides sharp acceleration at high rpm while maintaining easy handling at mid-range rpm.


The 7-sided silencer has a carbon fiber end and a tapered shape for a stylish and compact design.

The included sound diffuser can be detached and attached, allowing you to select the volume level that best suits your riding situation.


The lightweight titanium pipe contributes to further performance improvement.

The weight is about 60% lighter than the stock exhaust.


Silencer covers, glass wool, and carbon ends can be remade to support the user's motorcycle life.
